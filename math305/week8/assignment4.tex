\section{}

\#1) Prove the following by using mathematical induction.

\paragraph{}

For all n $\in \mathbb{N}$, $5^{2n}-1$ is a multiple of 8.

\paragraph{}

\begin{equation}
	\begin{aligned}
		(8\cdot3+1)^1-1=3\cdot8 \\
		8\cdot3+1-1=3\cdot8 \\
		8\cdot3=3\cdot8 \\
	\end{aligned}
\end{equation}

$\square$
